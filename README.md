# Production, Sales and Operations Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Production, Sales, and Operational performance using Excel and Power BI. The dashboard is designed to provide business-focused insights that support data-driven decision-making across manufacturing, sales, and operations.

The analysis evaluates production efficiency, profitability, downtime impact, resource utilization, and product-level performance.

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning and Preparation)
- Power BI (Data Modeling and Dashboard Development)

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Production Overview
- Total Bottles Produced: 22 Million
- Production Efficiency: 98.14%
- Damage Rate: 1.86%
- Total Downtime: 60,000 minutes
- Monthly production trend

- Shift-wise performance

### 2. Sales Overview

- Revenue by Sales Channel
- Revenue by Region

### 3. Operations Performance
- Downtime by Production Line

## Key Insights

- Production operations are stable with high efficiency and low damage rate.
- Downtime is concentrated in specific production lines and shifts.
- Retail is the dominant sales channel, indicating channel dependency.
- Revenue is geographically concentrated in Abu Dhabi and Dubai.
- The 5 Gallon product generates the highest revenue and profit.
- Energy consumption increases during peak production periods.

## Business Recommendations

- Diversify sales channels by strengthening online and subscription models.
- Reduce downtime through preventive maintenance and shift accountability.
- Improve energy efficiency through optimized production scheduling.
- Expand regional sales to reduce market concentration risk.
- Improve margin performance of lower-performing products.
- Optimize logistics through better route planning and transport mix.

## Expected Business Impact

- Improved production reliability
- Reduced operational costs
- Better energy and resource utilization
- Diversified revenue streams
- Stronger long-term operational sustainability
